WELCOME TO MY TEACHING PORTFOLIO!

I am Elena. I am passionate about art and interested in teaching visual arts. I dream of conveying my passion to students and helping them discover the power of art and its universal language. I like painting, drawing, sculpture, and digital media. Also, I love to code, drink coffee, shoot photos, and eat chocolate. I am a member of Phi Kappa Phi and NSLS. My recent success is a Magna cum Laude medal awarded by San Jose State University in November 2023.
